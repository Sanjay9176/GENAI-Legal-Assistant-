PROCEDURE FOR FILING A POLICE COMPLAINT (CRPC, 1973)

Follow this process to report a non-cognizable offence or lodge a general complaint.

Step 1: WRITE THE COMPLAINT
Write a detailed description of the incident including date, place, and names of people involved.

Step 2: SUBMIT TO POLICE
Submit the complaint to the Station House Officer (SHO) of the local police station. Obtain a copy with a receiving stamp.

Step 3: NON-COMPLIANCE
If police refuse to accept the complaint, send it to the Superintendent of Police or file a private complaint under Section 190 CrPC before the Magistrate.

Step 4: FOLLOW-UP
Keep copies of all correspondence and visit the police station periodically to check progress.
