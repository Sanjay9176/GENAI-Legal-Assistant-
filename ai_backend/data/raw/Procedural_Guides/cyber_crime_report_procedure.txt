PROCEDURE FOR REPORTING CYBER CRIME (INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY ACT, 2000)

Cyber crimes include hacking, identity theft, online fraud, stalking, and data breaches.

Step 1: COLLECT EVIDENCE
Take screenshots, URLs, emails, messages, or transaction IDs related to the offence.

Step 2: REPORT ONLINE OR OFFLINE
File a complaint at your nearest Cyber Crime Police Station or visit the official portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in

Step 3: INCLUDE DETAILS
Provide your name, contact details, and detailed description of the incident. Attach evidence copies.

Step 4: FIR REGISTRATION
For serious offences, request registration of an FIR under relevant IPC and IT Act sections.

Step 5: FOLLOW-UP
Track your case using the complaint ID or by contacting the investigating officer.
