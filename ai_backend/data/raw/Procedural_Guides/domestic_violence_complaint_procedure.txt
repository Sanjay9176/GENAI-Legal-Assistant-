PROCEDURE FOR FILING A DOMESTIC VIOLENCE COMPLAINT (PROTECTION OF WOMEN FROM DOMESTIC VIOLENCE ACT, 2005)

Women facing physical, emotional, sexual, or financial abuse can seek immediate protection.

Step 1: CONTACT AUTHORITIES
Approach the local police station, Protection Officer, or NGO for immediate help.

Step 2: FILE A COMPLAINT
Submit a written complaint to the Magistrate or through the Protection Officer.

Step 3: RELIEFS AVAILABLE
The court can issue:
- Protection Order (restraining the abuser)
- Residence Order (right to stay in shared home)
- Monetary Relief (maintenance)
- Custody Order (for children)

Step 4: INTERIM RELIEF
Emergency protection orders can be granted the same day if the situation is urgent.

Step 5: FOLLOW-UP
Keep copies of all applications and court orders for future reference.
