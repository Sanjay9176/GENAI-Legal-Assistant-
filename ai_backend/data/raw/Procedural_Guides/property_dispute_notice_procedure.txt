PROCEDURE FOR SENDING A LEGAL NOTICE IN PROPERTY DISPUTES (TRANSFER OF PROPERTY ACT, 1882)

Use this process when property rights or possession are disputed.

Step 1: VERIFY OWNERSHIP
Collect documents like sale deed, title, tax receipts, and encumbrance certificate to confirm ownership.

Step 2: DRAFT THE NOTICE
Include property details, nature of dispute, relief sought, and demand for action (e.g., vacate property, pay rent, or stop encroachment).

Step 3: SEND THE NOTICE
Send through a registered advocate using Registered Post with Acknowledgment Due (RPAD).
